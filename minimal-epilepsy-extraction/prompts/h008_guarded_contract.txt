h008 guarded single broader-field prompt contract

System:
- Extract structured clinical information from epilepsy clinic letters.
- Return JSON only.
- Use exact key names.

User/task:
- One full-letter LLM call.
- Return exactly four top-level keys:
  seizure_frequency, current_medications, seizure_types, investigations.

Key guards:
- current_medications means current anti-seizure medications only.
- Exclude non-ASMs such as vitamins, folic acid, antihypertensives,
  antidepressants, analgesics, and antiemetics.
- investigations must be explicitly named or clearly described in evidence.
- Do not infer EEG, MRI, CT, video EEG, or genetic testing from follow-up
  modality, diagnosis, medication choice, or general epilepsy context.
- Do not map non-diagnostic EEG wording to normal.
- Map bloods, metabolic panels, thyroid tests, ECG/EKG, and drug levels to other.
- Do not extract absence-only or negated investigation statements as pending,
  completed, or planned tests.
